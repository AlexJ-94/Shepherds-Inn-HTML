Rules:

Dress

Please dress children in appropriate clothes for play, keeping in mind that the children usually do art activities daily. We go outside on a regular basis, weather permitting. Please make sure children have appropriate clothing for outside play. On cold days children should have coats, hats, mittens, scarves, boots, and snow suits if necessary. Outside bag in case of an accident that would require a change.

Prescribed Diets

If your child has a prescribed diet by a source of medical care, we will do our best to accommodate it according to our menu plan. If unable, we would ask that you would provide items. If this is the situation, we must have a diet order and its duration specified in your child's files and posted in their room.
