We are happy to welcome you to Shepherds Inn Christian Daycare INC. We are a non-denominational Christian daycare.

We are currently licensed through Sherburne County Social Services for childcare and are taking children 16 months to 12 years old.

We are looking towards expanding to be licensed through Minnesota Department Of Human Services in the future to increase our license capacity.All children are welcome regardless of race, color, creed, national origin, religion, gender, or source of payment.
