Teaching Methods:

We develop our own curriculum with weekly themes and activities to help children enjoy the gift of learning. This is brought about by various methods of enhanced teaching. We use puppetry, story time, arts and crafts, large muscle activities, dance, exercising, performing arts, Christmas programs, along with many more creative ways to teach and help children learn and develop to be well rounded. Our activities are conducted with a Christian emphasis.
