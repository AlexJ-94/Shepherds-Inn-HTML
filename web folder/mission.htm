Our Mission:

The Mission of the Shepherds Inn Christian Daycare is to provide a safe, affordable, educational, and fun atmosphere for your child to grow on an individual basis. We encourage open communication between staff and parents to provide the best possible placement for your child to enjoy the gift of life.
