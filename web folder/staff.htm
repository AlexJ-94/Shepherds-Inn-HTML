Our Staff:

Our staff is qualified for child care. They are warm, loving and fun. We look forward to working with you and your child and providing the best care possible. It is our goal to create a pleasant, family-type atmosphere where your child can learn, be nurtured and grow. We have high standards in developing and maintaining a quality child care program. Our staff is prohibited from abusing prescription medications or being in any manner under the influence of a chemical that impairs their ability to provide our service to the children and their parents. The children are supervised at all times.
